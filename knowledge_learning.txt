Recommender systems are of three different types-
1. Content Based:- These are those systems which based on the similarities of content recommends the content. Here tags are created and based on the similarity of tags the recommender system works.
2. Collaborative Filtering:- This works on the basis of the user interests. Basically based on the similarities of user the contents are recommended.
3. Hybrid:- This is the combination of both Content Based and Collaborative Filtering.
